The Martyr's gallery and Museum

One of the seven urns containing the ashes of Udham Singh are kept in the museum The Martyr's gallery contains a number of paintings including some of political leaders and a painting of the inside of Jallianwala Bagh, showing a number of people dead on the ground. The addition to the painting of the Gurkha's was painted in at a later date. The names of those killed are not included.A portrait of Udham Singh is on display in the gallery.One of the seven urns containing his ashes are kept in the museum.
Using newspaper clippings and letters from Mahatma Gandhi, Rabindranath Tagore and others, 45 panels depicting the Amritsar massacre are displayed.
